Pastel Palettes

We at BITES OF BLISS wish to inculcate a healthy lifestyle for our clients through a wide range of health foods. To keep up with the requirements of leading a healthy lifestyle, none of our products have any added sugar, preservatives or emulsifiers and are either roasted or baked. We aim at providing our customers a meal in their pocket, an on-the-go snack, a healthy breakfast to begin your day or simply to include in your everyday diet, a pre or post workout meal, an evening or morning tea/coffee companion, an after meal healthy indulgence, a mid-meal healthy snack and options for accompaniments with your wines or dips and cheese boards.

1.Bun: The base and top layers, typically soft and lightly toasted, often made from wheat flour or sesame seeds.
2.Patty: The main protein, usually beef, chicken, or plant-based alternatives, which is seasoned and cooked to perfection.
3.Lettuce: Fresh, crunchy leaves like iceberg or romaine that add a crisp texture and freshness.
4Tomato: Sliced tomatoes, adding juiciness and a slight tang to the burger.
5.Cheese: A slice of cheese, often cheddar, American, or Swiss, melted over the patty for richness and flavor.
6.Onions: Raw, grilled, or caramelized onions, adding sweetness, sharpness, or savoriness.
7.Pickles: Sliced cucumbers that are pickled to give a tangy and crunchy contrast to the other ingredients.
8.Condiments: Sauces like ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or specialty sauces like BBQ or ranch, enhancing flavor.
9.Bacon: Optional crispy bacon strips that add smokiness and a satisfying crunch.
10.Additional toppings: Can include items like avocado, mushrooms, jalapeños, or fried eggs for extra flavor and texture.
